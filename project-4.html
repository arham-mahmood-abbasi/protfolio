LIRIX

This page contains the description of the project Lirix, which is a platform that provide the solutions for the distribution systems from order palcing to inventory management from picking to packing and delivering the order a full fleg solution for the distribution system.

Live Link

Project Overview

As the Team Lead for the LIRIX project, I was responsible for developing a comprehensive web application that streamlines the distribution process for our clients. The platform offers a range of features, including order placement, inventory management, and real-time tracking of shipments. My role involved collaborating with cross-functional teams to gather requirements, design user-friendly interfaces, and implement robust backend systems.

From system architecture to implementation, I played a pivotal role in ensuring the project's success. My contributions included designing the database schema, implementing RESTful APIs, and integrating third-party services to enhance functionality. I also focused on optimizing performance and ensuring scalability to accommodate future growth.
